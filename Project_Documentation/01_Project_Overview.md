# Project Overview: CultureConnect

**CultureConnect** is a web platform designed to preserve and promote cultural heritage through a digital marketplace and learning platform. It connects users with cultural artifacts and knowledge, fostering a community of cultural appreciation.

## Core Modules

### 1. Marketplace (Physical Products)

A platform for artisans and sellers to list traditional items.

- **Categories**: Traditional Clothing, Instruments, Arts & Decors.
- **Features**: Product browsing, search, cart, checkout, seller specific pages.

### 2. Learn Culture (Digital Learning)

An educational hub for cultural skills.

- **Categories**: Dances, Singing, Instruments, Art & Crafts.
- **Features**: Course listings, teacher profiles, video content (implied), enrollment.

### 3. User Roles & Dashboards

- **Public/User**: Browse products and courses, user profile, settings.
- **Seller**: Dedicated dashboard to upload products, manage inventory, view analytics, and customize their seller profile.
- **Teacher**: Dedicated dashboard to upload courses, manage classes, view student analytics, and customize their verified profile.
- **Admin**: High-level oversight, approval workflows for teachers, and platform analytics.

## Key Features

- **Authentication**: Secure Login/Signup with OTP and Forgot Password flows.
- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Specific routes for Sellers and Teachers to ensure data privacy and security.
- **Modern UI**: Polished interface with animations (GSAP, Rive), carousels, and responsive design.
